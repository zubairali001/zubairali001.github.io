Ballscapes – Ball Sort Puzzle

Ballscapes is a zen‑like colour sorting game developed in Unity. Players tap tubes to rearrange colourful balls, matching colours with as few moves as possible. Hundreds of handcrafted stages progress from casual warm‑ups to mind‑bending puzzles, with tube shapes and capacities changing to add variety. Daily challenges and progression unlock coins used to purchase skins and themes, while optional hints and extra tubes prevent frustration. Haptic feedback, a soothing soundtrack and unlimited time make it ideal for all ages, and everything plays offline.
